dsanson wrote:
> "Ask not [...] what you can do for your country."
>
> Maruku chokes on the [...], thinking it is a badly
> defined link of some sort.

You can escape (prefix with a backslash) the bracketed expressison in
such cases:

"Ask not \[...] what you can do for your country."

# maruku --version
Maruku 0.5.9

# echo 'Ask not \[...] what you can do for your country.' | maruku
<p>Ask not [&#8230;] what you can do for your country.</p>
