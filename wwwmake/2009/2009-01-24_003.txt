> I generate and show slideshows on my PC, and it displays fine.
> I ftp the files to a Linux-run =A0website, and the svg file shows up as
> XML in the background.

  I'd say you have to configure your webserver to set/send the correct
mime type for SVG. Here's the Apache configuration I use for the
slideshow.rubyforge.org site, for example [1].

  AddType image/svg+xml .svg

   That's it. Cheers.

[1] http://slideshow.rubyforge.org/svn/webby/content/.htaccess
