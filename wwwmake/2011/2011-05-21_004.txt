Hello,

 Ryan McIlmoyl has published a semantic version of the S6 HTML Blank
Template pack that lets you create slideshows in HTML5+CSS3+JavaScript
(w/jQuery).

<div class="presentation">
  <div class='slide cover'>
    <h1>Your Slide Title Here</h1>
    <ul>
      <li>Item One Here</li>
      <li>Item Two Here</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  Becomes:

<article class="presentation">
  <section class='slide cover'>
    <h1>Your Slide Title Here</h1>
    <ul>
      <li>Item One Here</li>
      <li>Item Two Here</li>
    </ul>
  </section>


   Find out more @ https://github.com/ryanmcilmoyl/s6

   Cheers.
