Hello Bruce,

    No panic. If you haven't changed anything it cannot break.

    Looking at your code at
http://bruce.darcus.name/presentations/rendition.html I guess your
slide templates and the JavaScript machinery are out of sync. A while
back the "magic" ids for CSS stylesheets got changed. Thus,

   <link ... media="projection" id="slideProj"/>
   <link ... media="screen" id="outlineStyle"/>
   <link ... media="print" id="slidePrint"/>

   should in your case read:

   <link ... media="projection" id="styleProjection"/>
   <link ... media="screen" id="styleScreen"/>
   <link ... media="print" id="stylePrint"/>

   Note, that, you can see the history of changes to S6 at
https://github.com/geraldb/s6/blob/master/history.markdown

   If you use the latest S6 JavaScript machinery, note, that you no
longer need the "magic" ids  for your stylesheets. Just use:

   <link ... media="projection" />
   <link ... media="screen" />
   <link ... media="print" />

   Hope that helps. All the best. Cheers.
