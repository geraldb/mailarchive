Hello J=F6rg,

   It depends on your template pack. Some do not include footers "out-
of-the-box", thus you need to change the template. If you use the S6
Blank template [1] you can use the footer and subfooter headers e.g.

<div class=3D"layout">
  <div id=3D"header"></div>
  <div id=3D"footer">
    <h1><%=3D @headers['footer'] %></h1>
    <h2><%=3D @headers['subfooter'] %></h2>
  </div>
</div>

 All the best. Cheers.
