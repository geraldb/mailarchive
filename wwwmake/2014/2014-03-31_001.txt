Hello,

  The Slide Show (S9) gem got a brother or sister, that is, let's
welcome the Hyper Book (H9) gem that lets you build hypertext books in
two versions, that is, an all-in-one-single-page version and a
multi-page version.

  It's still early - the good news the hybook gem shares (reuses) half
a dozen slideshow gems (pakman - template pack manager; markdown -
markdown wrapper n helpers; textutils - text filters, helpers and
more;  props - settings hierachies etc. ) and, thus, will also share
this mailing list/forum.

  The first books in the works built by Hyper Book (H9) include:

  - The Free World Beer Book [1]
  - The Free World Football Almanac [2]
  - The Free World Facts Book [3]

   More info @ github.com/hybook/hybook

  As always, everything is public domain (no copyright, no license). Cheers.

[2] github.com/openfootball/book
[3] github.com/openmundi/book
