Hello,

   If you follow along - most slideshow generators use

# Heading 1  or
## Heading 2

  for slide breaks or use a directive e.g.

!SLIDE

  or use an horizontal rule(r) e.g.

-----  or
- - -  etc

  Just stumbled on Bedecked [1] and it seems to have found what might
be an obvious new easy way (when looking back), that is, use two (or
lets say three) or more blank lines. Example:

# I'm a slide!


## I'm another slide!


## I'm a third!

 With some text.


![I'm a fourth slide with image only!](i/vienna.png)

 and so on.

  Have you seen any other options for slide breaks? What's your
preference? Do you want to see the "three-blank-lines-rule" get added
to the Slide Show (S9) tool? Let us know what you think.

   Cheers
