Hello,

> when i use google HTML5 Rocks templates
> ..
> how can i fix this?

  You have to change/fix the HTML template, that is, html5.html.erb
[1]

  Change:

   <% @slides.each_with_index do |slide,i| %>
     <div class="slide transitionSlide" id="<%= %Q(slide#{i+2}) %>">
       <header><%= slide.header %></header>
       <section>
         <%= slide.content %>
        </section>
     </div>
    <% end %>

   to use the new content_without_header template tag documented here
[2]. For example:

   <% @slides.each_with_index do |slide,i| %>
     <div class="slide transitionSlide" id="<%= %Q(slide#{i+2}) %>">
       <header><%= slide.header %></header>
       <section>
         <%= slide.content_without_header %>
        </section>
     </div>
    <% end %>


   Let me know if it works. Cheers.

[1] https://github.com/geraldb/slideshow-google-html5-slides/blob/master/html5.html.erb
[2] https://github.com/geraldb/slideshow/wiki/Template-Reference
