  Thanks for your kind words and thanks for reporting the bug.  The h1
heading got extracted and stored in the slide.header variable and
slide.content (was thus the content without the optional heading 1).

slide.content_without_header and slide.content now always holds the
complete slide content [1].

should work now (including h1 elements in your slide content).
