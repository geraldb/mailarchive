Hello,

   Just to let you know that using the free, open source wkhtmltopdf
tool [1] (available for Windows, Linux, Apple OS X) lets you create a
PDF from your slides with a single command line call e.g.

   $ wkhtmltopdf --print-media-type http://slideshow.rubyforge.org/tutorial.html
tutorial.pdf

   Note, however, that it only works for your print/outline view (and
not for the presenation mode that includes your gradient background of
choice. Sorry.)

   Cheers. Gerald

[1] http://code.google.com/p/wkhtmltopdf
