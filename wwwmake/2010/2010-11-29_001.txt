Hello Jason,

    Thanks for your kind words. To center text the easiest way is to
use CSS. The built-in template already has some CSS classes defined
for centering text, that is, center and fullscreen.

    You can use it like (using Textile markup):

    h1(fullscreen). Part 1 - What's REST?

    or

   h1(center). Part 1 - What's REST?

   Cheers.

PS: You can find the sample slideshow @ http://slideshow.rubyforge.org/rest.html
and the textile source @ https://github.com/geraldb/slideshow/raw/master/samples/rest.textile
