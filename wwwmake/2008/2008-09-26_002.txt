  The easiest way to get started is using the slideshow gem. Once you
have the slideshow gem installed just create a plain text file such as
(intro.textile):

title:  Slide Show (S9) 10-Minute Tutorial

h1. What's Slide Show (S9)?

yada yada

h1. Wiki-Style Markup Language =96 Markdown, Textile

yada yada


   And then run:

 slideshow intro

 That's it. Now you can open the generated intro.html page in your
browser. All the S6 machinery gets copied into the S6 folder.

  More info on the project site @ http://slideshow.rubyforge.org   and
 http://slideshow.rubyforge.org/tutorial.html

  Cheers.
