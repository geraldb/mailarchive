Hello,

  I've updated the worlddb gem [1] that includes models for places,
continent, countries, regions, cities and more. What's new in v2.0?

  The worlddb gem now includes new places and names tables, that is,
every continent, country, region, city is also a place w/ longitude
and latitude.

   The project also includes free open public domain plain text
fixtures [2] for all countries and territories in the world plus some
regions/state/provinces and some cities/towns/hamlets/etc.

   Use it like:

    at =3D Country.find_by! key: 'at'
    at.name
    # =3D> 'Austria'
    at.pop
    # =3D> 8_414_638
    at.area
    # =3D> 83_871

    at.regions.count
    # =3D> 9
    at.regions
    # =3D> [ 'Wien', 'Nieder=F6sterreich', 'Ober=F6sterreich', ... ]

    at.cities.by_pop
    # =3D> [ 'Wien', 'Graz', 'Linz', 'Salzburg', ... ]

 Note, the worlddb also supports tags and taggings:

   euro =3D Tag.find_by! key: 'euro'
   euro.countries.count
   # =3D> 17
   euro.countries
   # =3D> ['Austria, 'Belgium', 'Cyprus', ... ]

   flanders =3D Tag.find_by! key: 'flanders'
   flanders.regions.count
   # =3D> 5
   flanders.regions
   # =3D> ['Antwerpen', 'Brabant Wallon', 'Limburg', 'Oost-Vlaanderen', ...=
 ]
   flanders.regions.first.country.name
   # =3D> 'Belgium'

  Cheers.
