Hello,

   Your second question:

> And furthermore - what format is this?

   The format is "just" an extended version of the comma-separated values
(CSV) format. The beerdb. parser/reader supports two versions:

  The classic (all fields in a single-line) version and a "modern"
multi-line version. That's what this looks like:

[boston]
Boston Beer Co. (Samuel Adams)|Boston Beer Company, 1984
city:boston
Boston | MA 02130 // 30 Germania St.

   is basically the same as:

boston, Boston Beer Co. (Samuel Adams)|Boston Beer Company, 1984,
city:boston, Boston | MA 02130 // 30 Germania St.

  This is just a comment line:
   ####################################################


  Cheers.
