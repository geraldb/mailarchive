  Tip: For the very first time issue: 

   $ beerdb setup us --include ./us-united-states --worldinclude ./world.db 
     # -- note: you missed the us after setup

   $ beerdb up us --include ./us-united-states

  $ beerdb up at --include ./at-austria
  $ beerdb up de --include ./de-deutschland
