Hello,
   I see.  Here are some thoughts / ideas:  As it is now you can only
update the complete document (schedule) in the main repo. Of course,
you can always generate the schedule w/ the new results but that looks
like not such a great idea (because you will lose the comments,
white-space formatting, etc.)

 1)  Thus, you might be use search and replace w/ text patterns
(regex) and "patch" the schedule (instead of regenerating it every
time - this keeps your comments and white space formating etc. as is.

  2)  Another alternative is using the new football.csv format and
generate a list of matches w/ results in the new football.csv format
(it is **designed** on purpose for auto-generation).  See the Ligue 1
2013/14 [1] as an example e.g.

   Date,Team 1,Team 2,FT,HT

2013-08-09,Montpellier,Paris SG,1-1,1-0
2013-08-10,Bordeaux,Monaco,0-2,0-0
2013-08-10,Evian Thonon Gaillard,Sochaux,1-1,1-0
2013-08-10,Lille,Lorient,1-0,1-0
   ...

  Only downside now you have one extra file to maintain (and import).
Keep it up. Cheers.

[1] github.com/footballcsv/fr-france/blob/master/2013-14/1-ligue1.csv
