Hello Joe,

  FYI:  Note, to list all world cup teams you can use the events_teams
table (no need to go through the groups_teams join table and groups).
Example:

  select t.*
  from teams t
  inner join event_teams et on et.team_id = t.id
  inner join events e on e.id = et.event_id
  where e.key = 'world.2014'
