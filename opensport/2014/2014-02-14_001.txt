  Sorry my mistake. For the Premiere League you also have to include the 
teams from Wales, that is, Swansea City and Cardiff City. Thus, load first 
the teams from the europe repo [1] followed by the  teams and fixtures from 
the en-england repo. Example:

  $ sportdb setup teams --include ./europe --worldinclude ./world.db
  $ sportdb update --include ./en-england

  or using the Rakefile add:

 task :importsport => :importbuiltin do
  SportDb.read_setup( 'setups/teams',   './europe' )
  SportDb.read_setup( 'setups/all',     './en-england' )
 end 

  Good luck. Let me know if it works. Cheers.

[1] https://github.com/openfootball/europe
