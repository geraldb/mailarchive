Hello,

     Thanks for your interest in football.db.  The world-cup fixtures 
requires / depend on the national teams and leagues (stored in the 
national-teams [1] repo.

   Thus, try:

   $ sportdb setup   --include ./national-teams --worldinclude ./world.db 
   $ sportdb update --include ./world-cup
   
   Or use the official build script [2]: 

    rake build DATA=worldcup2014

  Let us know if it works. Cheers.

[1] github.com/openfootball/national-teams
[2] github.com/openfootball/build
