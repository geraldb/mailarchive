Hello,

   No worries.

> league.club

   It's a boolean flag if the team is a club (e.g. Manchester United)
if true or a national (selection) team (e.g.  Italy) if false  - it's
basically a reverse copy of the national flag and, thus, redundant.

> city.popm (I guess population in million but data doesn't seem so)

   This will change into its own table - it is the metropolitan
population  and not the city proper population    e.g.  New York might
have 10 m city proper and the metropolitan pop (popm( might have 15m
or more)

> region.nuts

    It's the eurostats code - kind of european iso region code

> country.net/motor/fifa

   net - is the tld  - the country domain e.g.  .at ,.jp etc.
   fifa  - is the fifa country code
   motor  - is the motor vehicle licence country code e.g.  A - for
Austria, D- Germany, etc.

> place.kind - What kind of choices do have totally?

   Best to look in the three country/region/city models. The should
have all a place_kind method e.g.

def place_kind
  if is_metro?
    'MTRO'
  elsif is_district?
    'DIST'
   else
    'CITY'
   end
end
