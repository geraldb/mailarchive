Hello,

   I have started adding standing calculations. Still early and rough. You 
can now calculate league and group standings. For auto-calculating all 
league standings use

    EventStanding.recalc!   and  for all groups
    GroupStanding.recalc!

   You can see the calc "engine" here [1] The "engine" calculates:

    - pos (ranking e.g. 1st place, 2nd place etc.)
    - played (matches played)
    - won
    - drawn
    - lost
    - goals_for
    - goals_against

   Cheers.

PS: New stats tables include:

 - AlltimeStanding / AlltimeStandingEntry 
 - EventStanding / EventStandingEntry
 - GroupStanding / GroupStandingEntry

[1] github.com/geraldb/sport.db.ruby/blob/master/lib/sportdb/calc.rb
