Hello,
    FYI: The easiest and recommend way to build yourself a copy is now:

   Step 0:   $ gem install sportdb

   Step 1:   $ git clone https://github.com/openfootball/eng-england
 # or download the zip archive

   Step 2:   $ cd eng-england

   Step 3:    $ sportdb build         # will use the ("in-situ") Datafile [1]

    That's it.

   To see and double check the db, try $ sportdb stats   or something.

 Cheers.
