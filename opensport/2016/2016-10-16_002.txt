Hello Ben,

  I see.

> My question is more how to build  the historical world cup matches using sportdb, either
> individually or all together.  Is this possible?

   What (files) gets included in the datasets is definited by the
setup files e.g. see /world-cup/setups [1]

   The default setup is all.txt [2] - thus, you will get all the
qualifiers for 2014 because they are listed in all.txt

   In your case you have two options:

  1) Try the setups/history.txt  [3] pre-configured setup  or
  2) Use your own setup file.

   To try the history.txt setup change the worldcup datafile to use
the history setup e.g.

world 'world.db', setup: 'countries'
football 'national-teams'
football 'world-cup'

   to

world 'world.db', setup: 'countries'
football 'national-teams'
football 'world-cup', setup: 'history'          ## NEW / ADDED  setup: 'history'

   Let me (us) know if that makes sense or works. Cheers.

[1] https://github.com/openfootball/world-cup/tree/master/setups
[2] https://github.com/openfootball/world-cup/blob/master/setups/all.txt
[3] https://github.com/openfootball/world-cup/blob/master/setups/history.txt
