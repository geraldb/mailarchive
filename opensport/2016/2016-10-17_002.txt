  The historic team for Yuguslavia is missing. Might also be the
Soviet Union and some others.


  Add the historic national teams [1][2] e.g.

football 'national-teams'
football 'national-teams', setup: 'history'

## Yes, it's ok to have two entries (they are different).

  Or as an alternative quick fix / workaround add the missing
(historic) countries to the datafile before the football entries e.g.

inline do
   Country.parse 'tch', 'Czechoslovakia', 'TCH', '1 km=C2=B2', '1'
   Country.parse 'yug', 'Yugoslavia', 'YUG', '1 km=C2=B2', '1''
   ## and some more
end

   Good luck. Cheers.
