Hello,
    thanks for reporting back.  Yes, sorry - if you use sportdb new
for now it always assumes starting from scratch / zero.

   What leagues / datasets do you want to include?

The recommended way is to write your own Datafile - example:

   football 'england', setup: '2019-20'
    football 'deutschland', setup: '2019-20'

   And than use in the same folder / directory:

   $ sportdb build

    That will download the zip datasets and build the database
(sport.db) from scratch / zero will all datasets listed in the
Datafile.

    Good luck. Cheers. Prost.
