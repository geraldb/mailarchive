Hello,

     Thanks for trying sport.db / football.db. 
   I double checked and I get the same weird dependency error. Not sure why 
really for now.
  The good news as a quick fix add this extra require to the top of your 
script:

    require 'sportdb/match/formats'

   Thus, resulting, for example in my example test script in:

    require 'sportdb/match/formats'
    require 'sportdb/source/footballdata'

    Footballdata.download( :at )

  That again works as advertised. Sorry for the trouble. Good luck.Cheers.
